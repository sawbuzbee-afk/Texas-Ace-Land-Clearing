Land Clearing in Navasota and Grimes County

Navasota is the county seat of Grimes County, sitting at the intersection of Highway 6 and Highway 105 in the Navasota River valley. Texas Ace Land Clearing serves Navasota and the surrounding Grimes County area from our home base in Montgomery — it's a straightforward run east on FM 1774 or Highway 105 to reach properties in the Navasota area.

Grimes County agricultural land around Navasota is some of the most historically productive in the region. Many properties feature a mix of sandy post oak uplands and clay-loam bottomland along the Navasota River and its tributaries. Brush encroachment is a persistent challenge for ranchers and landowners in this area, with yaupon holly, water oak, huisache, and native plum thickets taking over fence lines, creek banks, and old fields if not managed regularly.

Texas Ace Land Clearing brings the right equipment combination to every Navasota-area job. Our Hydro-Ax forestry mulcher on the CAT 275 track loader processes brush and light timber in place — ideal for pasture renovation where you want to avoid burning and keep the soil covered. For heavier timber and root clearing, the CAT 314D excavator handles large hardwoods efficiently and cleanly. The D4K2 dozer handles grading and land leveling once vegetation is cleared.

Whether you're clearing a new home site outside Navasota, renovating a cattle pasture that's gone to brush, or preparing a commercial lot along Highway 6, call Fred for a free on-site estimate. He'll come out, walk your property, and give you a written quote with no obligation.
