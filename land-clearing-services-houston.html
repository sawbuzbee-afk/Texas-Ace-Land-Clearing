Full-service land clearing for residential, agricultural and commercial properties across Montgomery, Grimes, Walker, San Jacinto and Waller Counties.

What We Clear

From half-acre residential lots to hundreds of acres of dense timber, our CAT-powered fleet handles every clearing challenge across Montgomery County and the surrounding region.

Overgrown Brush & Cedar

Dense cedar, yaupon holly, and invasive brush can take over Texas properties fast, making land nearly unusable. Our machines cut through thick, tangled growth in a fraction of the time manual methods require.

Dense Timber & Hardwood

Large oaks, pines, sweet gums, and mixed hardwoods are no match for our CAT 314D LCR Excavator equipped with a Dymax Tree Shear. We fell, stack, and process even the largest timber efficiently and safely.

Residential Lots

Whether you're building a new home or reclaiming your backyard from years of overgrowth, we prepare residential lots cleanly and carefully. We work around existing structures and property lines with precision.

Agricultural Acreage

Reclaim pastureland that has been overtaken by brush, cedars, or invasive species so you can return it to productive grazing or row crop use. Our dozer and track loaders handle large acreage jobs quickly and cost-effectively.

Commercial Sites

New commercial developments need a clean, graded pad before construction can begin. We handle large-scale commercial clearing projects efficiently, coordinating with contractors and meeting project timelines.

Pond & Creek Clearing

Overgrown pond banks and creek corridors can restrict water flow, increase flood risk, and harbor pests. We clear and reshape creek banks and pond edges while protecting drainage patterns and shoreline integrity.

Our Land Clearing Process

Every project follows a proven four-step process that keeps your job on schedule, on budget, and completed to a higher standard than you'd expect.

1

Site Assessment

Before any equipment rolls, Fred walks the property with the client to understand exactly what needs to go and what needs to stay. We identify any buried utilities, drainage features, septic systems, or trees the owner wants preserved, and we provide a free written estimate on the spot.

2

Equipment Selection

Based on vegetation type, acreage, and site conditions, we select the right combination of machines from our CAT fleet. Dense timber may call for the tree shear and excavator, while lighter brush is handled faster and more economically with our forestry mulcher or brush cutters.

3

Clearing

Our operators work systematically across the site, felling trees, grinding stumps, and clearing brush in efficient passes. Material is either mulched in place with the Hydro-Ax, stacked for burning where permitted, or loaded for haul-off depending on the project plan.

4

Cleanup & Grading

Once vegetation is cleared, our power box rake and dozer smooth the surface, remove roots, and establish a clean, even grade. We leave the property ready for construction, fencing, seeding, or whatever comes next, with no stumps, no piles of debris, and no loose root systems to trip on.

Why Hire a Professional Land Clearing Company?

Attempting to clear land on your own with a rented machine or manual labor is one of the most common — and most costly — mistakes property owners make. Without the right equipment and experience, what looks like a straightforward brush job can quickly turn into a multi-week ordeal. Rental equipment is often underpowered for real Texas brush and timber, leading to mechanical failures, stuck machines, and wasted days. There are also serious safety risks: falling trees, hidden stumps, unstable ground, and buried utilities create hazards that experienced operators are trained to identify and avoid.

Professional-grade CAT equipment makes an enormous difference in both speed and result quality. Our CAT D4K2 Dozer, CAT 314D Excavator, and CAT 289C Compact Track Loader are maintained to factory standards and operated by skilled professionals who clear land for a living. What might take a homeowner weeks of dangerous weekend work, our crew completes in a single day — and we leave the site cleaner, smoother, and better graded than any DIY effort could achieve. That translates directly into savings: fewer delays before construction begins, lower foundation prep costs, and no expensive equipment damage to pay for.

Beyond speed and safety, hiring Texas Ace Land Clearing means you have a single point of accountability. Fred Soucie oversees every job personally. We carry the proper liability coverage, we understand local burn permit requirements and environmental guidelines, and we won't leave a mess behind. When the job is done, you get a cleared, graded, and usable piece of ground — not a pile of slash, a field of stumps, or a damaged property.
