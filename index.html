Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Land clearing costs in the Montgomery County area typically range from $500 to $3,000+ per acre depending on vegetation density, terrain, acreage size and debris handling preference. Light brush on flat accessible land sits at the lower end. Dense hardwood timber with stumps, slopes or wet ground pushes higher. We provide free on-site estimates — call Fred and he'll walk your property with no obligation.

It depends on the project. For most jobs using our Hydro-Ax forestry mulcher, debris is chipped in place — no hauling needed. For dozer clearing or demolition generating large piles, we can burn on-site where permitted, bury material in low spots, or arrange haul-off. We'll discuss the best approach during your free estimate.

Most residential jobs of 1–5 acres are completed in 1–3 days. Larger commercial or agricultural projects take longer. Fred shows up on time every day and matches the right machine to your job. He'll give you a realistic timeline during your estimate — no surprises.

We serve Montgomery County, including The Woodlands, plus Grimes, Walker and San Jacinto Counties — generally anywhere within about an hour's drive of Montgomery, TX. Not sure about your location? Call 713-703-3452 — Fred will let you know right away.
