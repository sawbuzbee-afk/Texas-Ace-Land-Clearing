Land Clearing in Grimes County

Texas Ace Land Clearing serves Grimes County property owners from our base in Montgomery, TX — a short drive east on FM 1774 or Highway 105 puts us right into Grimes County's rolling post oak and pine woodlands. We've cleared properties throughout the county and understand the terrain, soils, and vegetation typical to the area.

Grimes County is predominantly rural agricultural land with properties ranging from small ranchettes to large cattle operations. The landscape is characterized by gently rolling terrain, sandy loam soils, creek drainages that feed into the Navasota River, and mixed hardwood-pine timber. Common clearing challenges in Grimes County include dense yaupon holly, water oak thickets, encroaching pine regeneration on old fields, and overgrown pastures that need to be returned to productive grazing.

Our CAT 314D excavator with tree shear handles the large hardwoods common around Navasota and Anderson, while our forestry mulcher on the CAT 275 track loader efficiently processes lighter brush and understory. For agricultural pasture reclamation, the D4K2 dozer clears large areas quickly and cost-effectively. We bring the right machine combination to every Grimes County job based on the specific conditions of your property.
