A Closer Look at Where We Work

Some areas we're in more often than others — here's a bit about what land clearing looks like in each.

Montgomery

As our home base, Montgomery is where it all started. We're out on Lake Conroe properties constantly — clearing brush for new builds, opening up waterfront views and prepping pads for barns and outbuildings. If you're nearby, we can usually swing by for a quick walkthrough the same week.

Free Estimate — 713-703-3452

Conroe

Conroe's mix of new subdivisions and older acreage keeps us busy with everything from full-lot clearing ahead of construction to thinning out overgrown tree lines along property fences. The sandy soil here drains well, which makes it some of the more forgiving ground we work on for grading and pad prep.

Free Estimate — 713-703-3452

The Woodlands

Around The Woodlands, most of our work is selective — thinning pines, clearing underbrush and opening up lots while keeping the mature trees that give the area its character. HOA guidelines are often part of the conversation, and we're used to working within them.

Free Estimate — 713-703-3452

Magnolia

Magnolia has some of the densest brush and cedar we deal with, especially on properties that have sat undisturbed for years. Our mulching equipment chews through that kind of overgrowth without leaving piles of debris behind, which matters on the smaller acreage lots common in this area.

Free Estimate — 713-703-3452

Spring

Spring is one of the busier corners of our service area, with a steady mix of residential lot clearing and small commercial site prep. Access can be tighter here than out near Montgomery, so we plan equipment delivery and staging carefully to keep jobs moving without disrupting the neighborhood.

Free Estimate — 713-703-3452

Tomball

Tomball sits right at the edge of our coverage area, and we head out there regularly for pasture reclamation and site prep on properties transitioning from rural to residential. Sandy loam soils make dozer work efficient, and we can often combine a Tomball job with stops in Magnolia or Cypress the same day.

Free Estimate — 713-703-3452
