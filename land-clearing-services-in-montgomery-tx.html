Your Local Land Clearing Company in Montgomery

Texas Ace Land Clearing isn't just a company that serves Montgomery, TX — we're based here. Fred Soucie operates out of 7148 North FM 1486, right in the heart of Montgomery County. When you call, you reach a neighbor who knows the local terrain, the soil conditions, and the vegetation patterns that characterize properties along the FM 1486 and FM 149 corridors.

Montgomery sits in the rolling pine and hardwood landscape of central Montgomery County, where land parcels often feature dense stands of loblolly pine, water oak, post oak, sweetgum, and yaupon holly understory. Properties near Lake Conroe can present wet, soft-soil conditions that require low-ground-pressure equipment and careful planning to avoid site damage. Fred has the experience and the right machines — rubber-tracked loaders and an excavator — to work these conditions without tearing up your property.

Whether you're clearing a new home site off FM 1486, reclaiming agricultural acreage along the Caney Creek watershed, cleaning up a neglected rural property near Old Plantersville Road, or clearing timber for a new pasture — Texas Ace has handled similar projects throughout Montgomery and can give you an accurate, no-surprise estimate based on real local knowledge.
