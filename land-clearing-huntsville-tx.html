Land Clearing in Huntsville and Walker County

Huntsville is the county seat of Walker County, located along I-45 north of Montgomery County. Texas Ace Land Clearing serves Huntsville and Walker County property owners, making the run up I-45 from our Montgomery base to take on clearing, site prep, and brush management projects across the county.

Walker County sits within the Piney Woods region of East Texas, and properties in and around Huntsville reflect that — dense loblolly pine and hardwood timber, rolling terrain, and soils that range from sandy uplands to heavy clay in bottomland areas. The Sam Houston National Forest borders Walker County to the south, and many private properties carry similar timber characteristics. Clearing this type of land requires the right mix of heavy equipment: our CAT 314D excavator with Dymax tree shear handles large pines and oaks efficiently, while the Hydro-Ax forestry mulcher on the CAT 275 processes undergrowth and scrubby timber without the need to haul debris off-site.

Common projects in the Huntsville area include clearing home sites and ranchette lots along FM 2821 and FM 1791 corridors, pasture reclamation on Walker County cattle operations, and site preparation for new commercial development along the I-45 corridor. We also handle old structure demolition and cleanup for properties being repurposed or redeveloped throughout Walker County.

Huntsville and Walker County property owners benefit from Texas Ace's track record in the broader region — Fred has worked extensively in the piney woods terrain that characterizes this area and understands the specific challenges of clearing and grading in these soil and timber conditions.
