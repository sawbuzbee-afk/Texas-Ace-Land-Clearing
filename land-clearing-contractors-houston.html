Who We Are

Texas Ace Land Clearing is an owner-operated land clearing company based in Montgomery, TX on North FM 1486 in the heart of Montgomery County. Fred Soucie founded the company and personally runs every job from initial estimate through final cleanup. When you hire Texas Ace, you're not dealing with a dispatcher or a subcontractor — you're working directly with the operator running the machine on your property.

Fred has spent years working on properties throughout Montgomery County and the surrounding region, clearing everything from half-acre residential lots in Magnolia to 200-acre timber tracts in Walker County. He's worked alongside general contractors, custom home builders, ranchers, and individual landowners — and he brings the same attention to detail to every project regardless of scale.

As a licensed contractor operating in Texas, Texas Ace carries the proper liability coverage and maintains equipment to manufacturer standards. Fred understands local burn permit requirements, drainage and environmental guidelines, and property line considerations that can trip up less experienced operators. His reputation in Montgomery County is built entirely on word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied landowners — a track record reflected in a 4.9-star Google rating across 49 reviews.

What Sets Texas Ace Apart

Owner on Every Job

Fred Soucie operates the equipment himself on every project. No subcontractors, no crews you've never met. The person you talk to on the phone is the person clearing your land.

Full CAT Fleet

Our fleet includes CAT excavators, CAT compact track loaders, a D4K2 dozer, Kubota mini excavator, and a full range of attachments. We bring the right machine to every job — not just what's available.

Reliable & On Time

Customers consistently note that Fred shows up when he says he will — often early. He keeps you updated, works straight through the day, and doesn't disappear between visits. No surprises.

Transparent Pricing

Free on-site estimates with no obligation. Fred walks your property before quoting so there are no surprises on the invoice. What he quotes is what you pay.

Local Knowledge

Fred has worked across Montgomery County for years. He knows the terrain — the clay soils, the creek drainage patterns, the East Texas hardwood species, and the local permit requirements. That local knowledge makes every job go smoother.

Clean Finish

Texas Ace doesn't leave until the job looks right. After clearing, we grade the surface, remove stumps and root systems, and leave you a clean, usable piece of ground — not a pile of slash and a field of trip hazards.
